Character Density Detection

Smart Detection: Instead of relying solely on keywords, this detects credits by identifying frames with high text density. Credits typically have 50-200+ characters per frame, while regular content has very few. This works regardless of which specific words appear in the credits.
Detect credits based on high text density rather than specific keywords (highly recommended)
When enabled, density detection is the main method and keywords are optional. When disabled, keywords are required but density boosts confidence.
Recommended: 20. Frames with this many characters or more are considered potential credits. Typical: Pre-credits ~5 chars, Credits ~100+ chars
Recommended: 3. How many consecutive frames must exceed the threshold to confirm credits (prevents false positives from occasional text overlays)
